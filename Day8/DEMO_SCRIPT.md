## üó£Ô∏è Voice Tips

- Speak clearly and at normal pace
- Pause after GM speaks to let it finish
- Be enthusiastic - you're playing D&D!
- React to dice rolls ("Yes!" or "Oh no!")
- Keep it natural and conversational
- Use descriptive actions ("I attack with my sword" not just "attack")
